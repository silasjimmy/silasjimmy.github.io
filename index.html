About me

Hi! My name is Silas Jimmy, a freelance software engineer based in Nairobi, Kenya. I have been in the tech industry for three years now, developing small scale to large scale software products with Vuejs and Flask. I also have experience in design, exploiting popular tools such as Figma and Canva to create user interfaces.

Currently, i am the CMO at Cyeco Africa Limited, a startup company that started in March 2023, with a goal of providing eco-friendly means of transport in Kenya. I am pasionate about developing mobile and web applications that connect businesses with their customers. I am at my best when i need to design solutions that meet the end user requirements by focussing on their needs and desires.

From the appearance, design and implementation, my goal is to ensure that the user interacts with the application seamlessly, while leaving a great impression that will keep bringing them back for more. I am a team player and a smart worker who is always ready to go beyond the comfort limits to guarantee customer satisfaction.

On my free time. i love doing research on different topics. However, my insatiable love of nature, particulary sunsets, has spiked my interest in photography which i usually do when travelling around the country. There are a lot of interesting bits about me that would fill a thousand pages. Lets connect and talk some more about tech and our gulty pleasures!.
